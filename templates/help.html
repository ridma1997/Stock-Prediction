Help - Stock Price Prediction

Getting Started

To use the Stock Price Prediction platform, follow these steps:

  1. Register/Login: Create an account or log in if you already have one.
  2. Get Stock Data:
    • Enter the stock ticker symbol (e.g., AAPL for Apple).
    • Select the data interval (Day, Week, Month, Quarter).
    • Choose the type of data (Profit or Interest).
    • Click the "Get Data" button to fetch and view the stock data.
  3. Profit Query:
    • Enter the desired profit target in pounds.
    • Select the period over which you want to achieve the profit (Day, Week, Month, Quarter).
    • Choose the data interval (Day, Week, Month, Quarter).
    • Click the "Get Stocks" button to see which stocks meet your profit criteria.
  4. Future Forecast Prediction:
    • Enter the stock ticker symbol.
    • Click the "Get Prediction" button to view future stock price predictions up to 2024-12-31.
  5. Logout: Click the "Logout" button when you are done.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact support at support@example.com.
